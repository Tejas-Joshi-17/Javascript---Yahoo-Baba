Google Chrome Console :-

    1) console.log()       :-   For Testing
    2) document.write()    :-   For Production
    3) console.clear()     :-   Clear the Console
    4) console.warn()      :-   Show Warning
    5) console.error()     :-   Show Error 
    6) console.time()      :-   Time Count Started
    7) console.timeEnd()   :-   Time Count Ended
