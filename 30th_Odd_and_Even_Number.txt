Odd & Even Number :-

1) Odd Number less than equal to 10 :-

    document.write("Odd Number less than 10 :- ")
    for(var a=1; a<=10; a++){
        if(a%2 != 0)
            document.write(a + "  ")
    }

2) Even Number less than equal to 10 :-

    document.write("Even Number less than 10 :- ")
    for(var a=1; a<=10; a++){
        if(a%2 == 0)
            document.write(a + "  ")
    }
